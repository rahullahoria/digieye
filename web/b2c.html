Business to Client

Our home is a place where we feel nurtured and secure with our family.But being surrounded by some unknown workers make us feel endangered.So to make your home more safe and secure for your family, you can register your domestic worker with DigiEye and get him/her verified with us. Because a home is where we should feel secure and comfortable.

What you need to send us- You just send us your contact details on digieye@shatkonlabs.com like your name and mobile no.Our Customer Executive will call you for further information.


Our Charges- DigiEYE take only 350Rs. + Service Tax for doing all verification process. Moreover if there is some suspicious activity done by the worker we will inform you by call, SMS and E-mail about such activity of that worker.

How it works- DigiEYE is the blue collar job authentication provider platform for all type of domestic works like cooking, cleaning, housekeeping, plumbing, electrician, driver, baby care, elderly care etc.It is highly committed to deliver best of the authentication for the customer. For this purpose, to provide a trustworthy, honest and experienced worker, DigiEYE has stringent process and specifications related to the domestic worker.
DigiEYE checks various aspects of a potential worker before recruiting him, so that she/he can deliver the best of the authentication.

Collection of all the documents ( like Pan Card, Voter ID Card, Aadhar Card, Passport, Driver’s License )

Documents and Information Verification

Identity and local & hometown address verification

Character reference checks like criminal history where He/She working Last

Previous employment checks
